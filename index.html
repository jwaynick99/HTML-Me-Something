Meet Gus.

Gustavo Franklin Waynick

Gus is my good boy. We've got two other dogs in our household, but Gus was my first dog. We've been together since before me and my wife were living together. Let me introduce him to you.

Gus had an accident.

He's not the most graceful beast.

When gus was 2 years old, we were living in an upstairs apartment, and Gus took a spill down a flight of stairs. This was when we found Gus had hip dysplasia. This had him down and out for a few months, and I, unfortunately did not have the money to pay for his procedure. We did daily therapy at home, and after awhile Gus's gait had improved, and now you wouldn't even know he had an injury.

Gus loves his siblings.

Even the human ones.

As noted above, Gus has two dog siblings, and in the last 2 years he now has 2 human siblings. (AKA my children) Gus is great with kids and other dogs. He's an old soul and would rather lay around with the others than do much else. This makes him our perfect family dog.

Gus is just a good boy

The Best Boy

Gus is the best dog in the whole world. He is better than your dog. I will not be accepting arguments on this matter. Hopefully, he will have many more years with me and my family.
